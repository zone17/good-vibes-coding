What We Do

AI that works for your
business, not against it.

Most small businesses are paying too much and doing too much by hand. We fix that with AI. No new hires. No new software subscriptions.

AI Automation & Efficiency

We plug AI into your day-to-day: sales, customer service, marketing, back office. Those manual tasks eating your time and budget? We automate them. Things that used to take hours happen in seconds, so your team can focus on the work that actually matters.

Custom Tools That Replace Expensive Software

Paying $500/month for software your team barely uses? We can build you a custom tool that does exactly what you need for a fraction of that cost. AI has made this possible in a way it wasn't even two years ago. Every vendor you drop is money back in your pocket, every month.

AI Agents That Run Your Operations

Imagine telling an AI "process today's orders" or "follow up with that customer" and it just does it. We build AI agents that know your business, follow your rules, handle multi-step tasks on their own, and know when to hand things back to a human.

Platform & Infrastructure Engineering

The behind-the-scenes plumbing that keeps software running: servers, deployment, monitoring, the stuff nobody sees until it breaks. We set it up right so your product stays fast, your team ships faster, and problems get caught before your customers notice.

MVP & Product Development

Got an idea for an app or product? We take it from concept to something real, using AI to move fast without cutting corners. Built clean so it can grow with you, at a price that doesn't require investor money.

Small Business & Community Tech

Not every business needs an enterprise solution. Most need something that just works, doesn't cost a fortune, and grows with them. Websites, booking systems, data dashboards, and automations built around how you actually operate.
